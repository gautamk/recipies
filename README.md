#### Instructions

- Peel ginger
- Peel garlic
- Weigh ginger and garlic on a 1:1 ratio
- Add 2% by weight Salt 
- Chop and add to jar to ferment
- Wait 1 - 2 weeks 
- Puree to smoothness of likeness
- Store in fridge
